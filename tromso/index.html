Discover Amazing places in Norway

Tromsø is a city in northern Norway, located 350 km north of the Arctic Circle. Most of the city is located on the island of Tromsø, connected to the mainland by a bridge. Tromsø is often referred to as the "Gateway to the Arctic" and the "Capital of the North".
