Discover Amazing places in Jeju-si

Jeju-si is a city in South Korea located on Jeju Island, which is famous for its picturesque landscapes and mild climate. The city is surrounded by volcanic landscapes and famous sites such as Mount Halla and Mansangul Cave. Jeju City is often called the "Hawaii of South Korea" due to its clubs and rich culture.

Sangumburi Crater

Sangumburi Crater is a unique volcanic crater on Jeju Island that is renowned for its outstanding natural beauty. This place is surrounded by dense forests and flowering plants, making it ideal for walking and enjoying nature.

Hallasan National Park

Hallasan National Park is located around Mount Halla of the same name, the highest in South Korea. Here you can see stunning views, hiking trails of varying difficulty levels and unique flora and fauna typical of Jeju Island.
